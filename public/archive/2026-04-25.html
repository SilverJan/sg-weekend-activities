Lyrids Meteor Shower — Peak Weekend

One of the oldest known meteor showers — first recorded in 687 BC — peaks this weekend. Expect up to 10–20 meteors per hour in the early hours. Head to a dark, open sky away from city lights between 2 AM and 5 AM. Changi Beach Park facing the strait or Labrador Nature Reserve are popular viewing spots.

📅 Sat night into Sun morning, 25–26 Apr · Best viewing: 2:00 AM – 5:00 AM

📍 Changi Beach Park (Changi) · Labrador Nature Reserve (Labrador Park MRT)

The newly revamped Malay Heritage Centre reopens on 25 April with an all-new storyline "Dari Rantau Ke Rumah" (From the Region to Home), tracing how Malay communities across the Nusantara shaped Singapore.
